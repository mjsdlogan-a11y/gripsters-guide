The Process

How Gripsters Guide works

From blank slate to bookable itinerary in minutes, built around how you travel — not how everyone else does.

01

Build your profile

Your pace, interests, mobility preferences, and what you actively want to avoid. One profile, remembered forever.

02

Describe your dream trip

As vague or specific as you like. "Three weeks in Japan, no package tours, good food" is plenty to start.

03

Refine in conversation

Your AI planner drafts a full itinerary then listens as you tweak it. Change anything, instantly.

04

Book with confidence

Flight and hotel links curated to your preferences, with free cancellation flagged and walking distances noted.
